1. Who is Popeye?

Popeye the Sailor Man is a fictional character created by Elzie Crisler Segar. He first appeared in the King Features comic strip Thimble Theatre on January 17, 1929.

2. Key Facts and Friends

  • First Appearance: January 17, 1929
  • Love Interest: Olive Oyl
  • Arch-Rival: Bluto (Brutus)
  • Favorite Food: Spinach
  • Famous Friend: J. Wellington Wimpy
